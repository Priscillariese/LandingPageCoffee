We are also known for our specialty coffees, which are carefully prepared with selected high-quality beans and precise preparation techniques.

Another popular option is Hario V60 coffee, which is prepared with a cone-shaped paper filter and results in a clean, smooth drink, highlighting the sensory notes of the beans.
